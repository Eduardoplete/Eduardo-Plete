Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is renowned for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and the romantic Seine River. Explore its charming streets, enjoy world-class dining, and immerse yourself in its rich history and culture.

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces, also known as *Hagdan-Hagdang Palayan*, are ancient, hand-carved terraces in the Philippines. They showcase stunning agricultural ingenuity and breathtaking landscapes, earning recognition as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano, located in Albay, Philippines, is renowned for its perfect cone shape. It’s a breathtaking natural wonder, offering scenic views, thrilling hikes, and rich cultural significance.

Boracay

Boracay is a tropical paradise in the Philippines, famous for its powdery white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and water activities like snorkeling, windsurfing, and parasailing.
